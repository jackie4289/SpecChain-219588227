# Manual Specification

## Requirement ID: FRM-01
- Description: [Donation prompts should be optional, and closing one should never stop someone from starting a session.]
- Source Persona: [P1 - Users on a budget]
- Traceability: [Derived from review group G1]
- Acceptance Criteria: [Given someone closes a donation prompt while browsing or right before playback, when the prompt disappears, then they stay in the app and can start the selected session without paying or creating an account.]

## Requirement ID: FRM-02
- Description: [The app should stay free of third-party ads while people browse and while sessions are playing.]
- Source Persona: [P1 - Users on a budget]
- Traceability: [Derived from review group G1]
- Acceptance Criteria: [Given someone spends 15 minutes moving through the home, course, and player screens and completes one session, when that flow ends, then no display, video, or audio ad has appeared.]

## Requirement ID: FRM-03
- Description: [People should be able to browse sessions by common emotional needs, including Anxiety, Stress, Panic, Grief, Anger, and Sleep.]
- Source Persona: [P3 - Users who seek emotional reset]
- Traceability: [Derived from review group G3]
- Acceptance Criteria: [Given someone opens the needs-based area, when it loads, then Anxiety, Stress, Panic, Grief, Anger, and Sleep appear as separate options and each one opens its own list of relevant sessions.]

## Requirement ID: FRM-04
- Description: [People should be able to save a session as a favorite and start it again from the home screen in two taps or fewer.]
- Source Persona: [P3 - Users who seek emotional reset]
- Traceability: [Derived from review group G3]
- Acceptance Criteria: [Given someone has already saved at least one favorite, when they open the home screen, then a Favorites section is visible and a saved session can be started in no more than two taps.]

## Requirement ID: FRM-05
- Description: [The Beginner course should guide people in order and show the next unfinished lesson when they come back.]
- Source Persona: [P4 - The Habit Builder]
- Traceability: [Derived from review group G4]
- Acceptance Criteria: [Given someone finishes lesson 1 in the Beginner course, when they reopen that course, then lesson 1 is marked complete and moves onto lesson 2 which is shown as the next lesson.]

## Requirement ID: FRM-06
- Description: [People should be able to set daily reminders for a specific local time and turn it on or off in settings.]
- Source Persona: [P4 - The Habit Builder]
- Traceability: [Derived from review group G4]
- Acceptance Criteria: [Given someone sets a daily reminder for 21:00 local time and enables it, when the reminder is saved, then that time is stored and one notification is scheduled for 21:00 each day until the reminder is turned off.]

## Requirement ID: FRM-07
- Description: [The Sleep area should list sleep meditations, sleep stories, and sleep sounds as separate sections.]
- Source Persona: [P2 - Bedtime Calming Listener]
- Traceability: [Derived from review group G2]
- Acceptance Criteria: [Given someone opens the Sleep section, when it loads, then sleep meditations, sleep stories, and sleep sounds appear as separate entries.]

## Requirement ID: FRM-08
- Description: [Sleep audio should support repeat and track queues without showing a blocking streak or completion popup between tracks.]
- Source Persona: [P2 - Bedtime Calming Listener]
- Traceability: [Derived from review group G2]
- Acceptance Criteria: [Given someone queues two sleep tracks or turns on repeat for one track, when the first playback ends, then the next track starts or the same track restarts automatically and no blocking popup interrupts the audio.]

## Requirement ID: FRM-09
- Description: [A session should keep playing for at least 10 minutes with the screen off and headphones connected, without crashing or stopping unexpectedly.]
- Source Persona: [P5 - Power Users]
- Traceability: [Derived from review group G5]
- Acceptance Criteria: [Given someone starts a session, turns the screen off, and keeps wired or Bluetooth headphones connected, when 10 minutes pass, then audio is still playing, the app should not crash, and playback controls still respond after reopening the app.]

## Requirement ID: FRM-10
- Description: [A signed-in user's streak and course progress should follow them across devices and come back after they sign in again.]
- Source Persona: [P5 - Power Users]
- Traceability: [Derived from review group G5]
- Acceptance Criteria: [Given a signed-in user completes a course lesson on device A, when that same account is opened on device B or signs in again on the original device, then the updated streak count and completed lesson appear within 60 seconds after sync finishes.]
